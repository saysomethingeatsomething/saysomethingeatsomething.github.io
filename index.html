Homework for US

a listening project.

That last exam was rough. Real talk, America: if we don’t want to fail, we have to do some serious make-up work.

America, your assignment:

Let's pop our bubbles and listen outside our geographic comfort zones. If you’re traveling for the holidays, go out into the community and listen to hopes and worries of strangers. How? We suggest food.

Here's the plan.

  1. Take some candy or warm apple cider and stand outside the supermarket, on Main Street, or wherever people are.
  2. Offer something to eat in exchange for hearing what a stranger is hoping for in their community.
  3. Listen and empathize. Remember, we are not here to argue. This is not a political discussion.
  4. Share with #AmericaListens.

Ready? Let's do it.

Commit to finish your homework, and assign it to a friend too.
