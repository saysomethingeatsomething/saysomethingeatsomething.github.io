Homework for US,

a listening project.

We’ve been sitting in the back of class all semester, chatting with our friends. Now we have some make-up work to do: pop our bubbles and listen to people outside our own echo-chambers.

America, your assignment:

If you’re travelling for the holidays, go out into the community and listen to hopes and worries of strangers. How? We suggest food.

Here's the plan.

  1. Take some candy or warm apple cider and stand outside the supermarket, on Main Street, or wherever people are.
  2. Offer something to eat in exchange for hearing what a stranger is hoping for in their community.
  3. Listen and empathize. Remember, we are not here to argue. This is not a political discussion.
  4. Share with #hw4us.

Ready? Let's do it.

Commit to finish your homework, and assign it to a friend too.
